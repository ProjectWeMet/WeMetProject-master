How do you add your project?


  • Start building your project

    Through Freelance, you can build your project as you want, enter the project details and the expected budget, so that your project will be reviewed and published for free on Freelance. After that, the best registered freelancers will submit different offers for you to choose the right offer for you and start implementing your project

  • An independent site that guarantees your rights

    When you deal with a freelancer through a freelance site, the site remains a mediator between you and the freelancer who implements your project, and only when the project is fully implemented is the amount transferred to the account of the freelancer who implemented your project.

  • Tips for a successful business

    • Enter the project details accurately
    • Fill in all the fields and provide examples of what you want
    • Divide the project into several small phases
